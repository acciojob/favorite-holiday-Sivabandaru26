Paris, France


Paris, the capital city of France, is known as the "City of Light" and is one of the most popular tourist destinations in the world. It is famous for its rich culture, art, fashion, and romantic atmosphere.

Top 5 Attractions

  1. Eiffel Tower – A symbol of Paris and one of the most iconic landmarks in the world.
  2. Louvre Museum – The largest art museum, home to the famous Mona Lisa.
  3. Notre-Dame Cathedral – A masterpiece of French Gothic architecture.
  4. Champs-Élysées – One of the most famous avenues, known for shopping and cafés.
  5. Sacré-Cœur Basilica – A stunning white basilica located on Montmartre hill.

Must-Try Foods

  • Croissants – Flaky and buttery pastries best enjoyed with coffee.
  • Macarons – Colorful and delicate sweet treats.
  • Baguette – A traditional French bread with a crispy crust.
  • Crêpes – Thin pancakes filled with sweet or savory ingredients.
  • French Cheese – A wide variety of cheeses, each with unique flavor.
